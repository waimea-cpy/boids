Boids

A classic algorithm that demonstrates emergent behaviour. A number of 'boids' follow very simple rules, reacting to their close neighbours. But this results in group behaviour similar to birds flocking.

Notes:

  • Boid Count - Total number of boids
  • Neighbourhood - Radius of a boid's 'neighbourhood'
  • Speed - Overall speed of all boids
  • Separation - Urge to avoid crowding neighbours
  • Coherence - Urge to stay close to neighbours
  • Alignment - Urge to head the same way as neighbours
  • Visualise - See neighbourhood of a boid and fear
  • You can click to scare boids away!
